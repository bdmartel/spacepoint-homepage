Architects of the Next Frontier

Visionaries with decades of experience across human spaceflight, planetary science, and engineering innovation — dedicated to accelerating permanent human presence on the Moon, Mars, and beyond.

Human Spaceflight

Dr Steve Swanson

From commanding the International Space Station to conducting 5 spacewalks, Steve's 3 missions to space account for over 197 days orbiting Earth. His career embodies the human spirit pushing beyond the bounds of Earth.

Planetary Science

Dr Ben Weiss

An internationally recognized leader in planetary science, Ben serves as a key leader and team member of multiple NASA missions driving robotic exploration across our solar system.

Engineering

Matt Dethlefsen

A lifelong passion for mechanical engineering and a track record of innovation in rocket technology, Matt is instrumental in driving industry-leading advances in aerospace and space exploration.
